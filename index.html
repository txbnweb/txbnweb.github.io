About us

Established in 2023, Thanh Xuan Media is a Vietnamese television channel operated completely by volunteers.

We offer a unique blend of shows tailored to Vietnamese viewers, featuring news magazine, special events, documentaries, entertainment and educational content.

With a powerful distribution network, our mission is to preserve and promote the richness of Vietnamese culture. We are committed to providing Thanh Xuan Media free of charge, with 6 hours of new programming every Sunday.

Highlights

  • Arts & Culture News - a fascinating look at the Vietnamese culture and lifestyle.
  • Vietnamese and international movies
  • Cartoons
  • General entertainment
  • Coverage of the latest sports events from around the world
